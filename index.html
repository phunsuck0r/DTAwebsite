About DTA

Engineering Excellence, Delivered with Integrity

DTA Electrical Mechanical is a trusted provider of comprehensive electrical and mechanical engineering services for commercial and industrial sectors across the East Midlands and beyond. With over 20 years of industry experience, we have built a reputation for quality workmanship, safety compliance, and customer satisfaction.

What We Do

Our Services

From design and installation to testing and maintenance, we provide end-to-end electrical and mechanical solutions for commercial and industrial clients.

Electrical Installations

Complete electrical installation services for new builds, refurbishments, and upgrades — from distribution boards to full wiring systems.

Mechanical Engineering

Commercial boiler installations, heating plant replacements, pressurisation systems, pipework modifications, and full plant room refurbishments.

Testing, Inspection & Commissioning

Comprehensive electrical testing, inspection, and certification services including EICR, PAT testing, thermographic surveys, and full system commissioning.

Maintenance & Repairs

Planned preventative maintenance and responsive repair services to keep your electrical and mechanical systems in optimal condition.

Emergency Power Systems

Design and installation of backup generators, UPS systems, and emergency lighting to ensure uninterrupted power supply.

Heating Plant Replacement

Complete removal and replacement of commercial boiler plant, circulation pumps, expansion vessels, and associated controls — delivered in phases to maintain continuity of service.
